Multiple Channel For Marketing

Email Template

Email marketing campaign is an email sent from you to one or more customers or segments. A successful email marketing campaign will get the users to act, engage, and help you get more leads and sales.

Sms Template

SMS Marketing is considered as a top-rated result-producing marketing method, making it the mobile channel that offers the widest reach possible. You can easily integrate a SMS provider of your choice from UseNDot dashboard.

Web Push

Web push campaign are notification based campaign where notifications are sent to a user via desktop web and mobile web. These alert-style messages slide in at the top or bottom right-hand corner of a desktop screen or appear on a mobile device in a way identical to push notifications delivered from apps.
